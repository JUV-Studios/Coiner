United States of America
American 1 Cent 2010:

American Dollar - 1 cent (Penny)- Back

This weighs 2.5 grams and measures 19.05mm across

First issued in 2010 and emblematic of Lincoln’s preservation of the United States as a single and united country. It features a union shield with 13 vertical stripes and our national motto in a horizontal bar above. A banner drapes across the front.

American Dollar - 1 cent (Penny) - Front

1909 - 2020

Shows the familiar likeness of President Lincoln featured since 1909.

American 5 Cent 1996:

American Dollar - 5 cent (Nickel) - Back

This weighs 5 grams and measures 21.2mm across

A representation of Monticello, President Thomas Jefferson's Virginia home.

American Dollar - 5 cent (Nickel) - Front

1938-2003

The portrait of Thomas Jefferson, the 3rd president of the U.S. from 1801 to 1809.

American 5 Cent 2014:

American Dollar - 5 cent (Nickel) - Back

This weighs 5 grams and measures 21.21mm across

Features the classic rendition of Monticello originally made by artist Felix Schlag in 1938, but with greater detail and relief added in the dome, balconies, door, and windows.

American Dollar - 5 cent (Nickel) - Front

2006-2020

Shows, since 2006, the Thomas Jefferson likeness based on a Rembrandt Peale portrait completed in 1800. Peale’s portrait was the basis for most of the images of Jefferson made during his lifetime.
